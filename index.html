What is Food Myth Buster?

Food Myth Buster is a small web app that turns confusing food claims into clear answers backed by real science.

Each myth page combines a verdict, explanation, linked studies, and community feedback so you can see not just what is true, but why.

🔬
Evidence first
Verdicts are built around peer‑reviewed studies and official guidelines, not opinions.
📚
Myth library
Browse myths by category, verdict, or search terms when you hear something suspicious.
👥
Community input
Users can submit myths, bookmark favourites, and vote on which explanations are most helpful.
